# Business Metrics

---

## Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|------|----------|
| Gross Revenue | SUM(line_total) |
| Net Revenue | SUM(net_line_revenue) |
| COGS | SUM(line_cost) |
| Gross Profit | Net Revenue - COGS |

---

## Sales Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|------|----------|
| Total Transactions | Count of completed transactions |
| Units Sold | SUM(quantity) |
| AOV | Net Revenue / Transactions |

---

## Inventory Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|------|----------|
| Inventory Turnover | Sold / Avg Stock |
| Stockout Rate | % of zero stock |

---

## Customer Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|------|----------|
| CLV | Revenue per customer |
| Repeat Rate | Customers with >1 purchase |

---

## Marketing Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|------|----------|
| Conversion Rate | Sessions → Purchases |
| Cart Abandonment | Add-to-cart vs purchase |
